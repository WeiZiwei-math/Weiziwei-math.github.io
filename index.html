Study Interests

    I am interested in the intersection of algebraic geometry, representation theory and mathematical physics, especially quantum groups, quiver varieties and mathematical gauge theory(But I know very little about them for now). For now the topic that I am most interested in is the construction of Coulomb branches by Braverman-Finkelberg-Nakajima. I am also interested in semi-classical & microlocal analysis and many other mathematics inspired by theoretical physics.
